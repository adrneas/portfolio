## Problem

Product teams were spreading research inputs across spreadsheets, documents, and meetings. The proposal organized tracking into a single surface without turning it into a generic dashboard.

## System

The interface prioritizes density, hierarchy, and comparison. Components were designed for repeated reading: compact lists, status markers, predictable filters, and evidence areas kept close to their context.

## Learning

The project reinforced the importance of treating internal tools as real work environments. Less decoration, more rhythm, legibility, and small decisions that reduce everyday friction.
