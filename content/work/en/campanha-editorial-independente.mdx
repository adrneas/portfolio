## Direction

The campaign starts with a question: how can a small publication feel precise without pretending to have a scale it does not have? The answer was to make limitation part of the language.

## Rollout

The system uses typographic contrast, image cuts, and a short palette with sharp accents. Digital pieces work as small visual summaries, moving readers toward the full text.

## Outcome

The project creates presence without depending on volume. The campaign can vary formats while keeping a recognizable signature.
