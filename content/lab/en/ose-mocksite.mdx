## About

This is a redesign project for the Open Source Ecology website, and it works as a study in page architecture and institutional narrative.

Since I discovered OSE's proposal, I have been fascinated by it: a collaborative form of learning, a tangible value proposition, and, above all, open source.

The idea was to organize the information into a clear structure, with editorial rhythm and reusable blocks.

The project works as a prototype for digital presence: quick to navigate, direct in its message, and flexible across different sections.

Their original website is here, in case you are interested: https://www.opensourceecology.org/.
