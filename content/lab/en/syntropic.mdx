## About

This has been a dear little project of mine. As I studied syntropic agriculture, I started wanting to materialize this knowledge and make it available in a more tangible way for the people I talked with about the subject.

Syntropic starts from the idea of guided growth, flow, and regeneration to build a small-scale visual experience.

As an experiment, it tests how motion, color, and composition can suggest a living system without relying on excessive explanation, seeking to empower and enable syntropic agriculture projects with real information.
