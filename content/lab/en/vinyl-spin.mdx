## About

I made Vinyl Spin for my DJ friend. The idea was to make it easier for him to generate the videos he uses to illustrate the tracks he has been sharing on Instagram :)

It uses the record image as a starting point for a simple, tactile interaction, preserving textures and the possibility of choosing the rhythm.
