## About

I love games! Here the idea was to build a campaign-format game based on chess.

Unlike traditional chess puzzles, this project has a board-generation engine that adapts to the rules I set for each stage; in other words, the system dynamically builds stages while guaranteeing solvability and unique challenges.

Pieces have "special powers" and certain board squares can have different effects. It is a WIP that I pick back up from time to time to play around with :).
