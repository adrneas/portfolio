## About

The idea here was to assemble a simple, dynamic system for organizing timers. It organizes work into simple cycles, with a visual layer to track progress, breaks, and return.

The project treats focus as an experience of rhythm, not only as a timer.

The interface aims to stay direct, with low friction to start a cycle and enough clarity to understand the current state.

I also wanted to test sharing here. Built your sequence of exercises and rests? Send it to that friend of yours!
