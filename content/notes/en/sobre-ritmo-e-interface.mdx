Dense interfaces do not need to feel heavy. Often, what is missing is not empty space, but rhythm: enough repetition to guide, enough breaks to keep attention.

When I think about product work, I try to look at the screen as an editorial page for continuous use. The user is not only reading; they are returning, comparing, looking for signals, and making small decisions.
